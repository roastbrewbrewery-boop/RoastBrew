Our Story

Roast & Barrel Brewery is an innovative craft brewery located in Baguio City, Philippines, specializing in coffee-infused craft beers. Combining the best coffee beans from Benguet and Mountain Province with small-batch brewing techniques, our beers offer unique, high-quality beverages that appeal to beer lovers and coffee enthusiasts.

From traditional lagers to innovative IPAs, every sip tells a story of dedication and craftsmanship. Join us in celebrating the rich tradition of brewing with a twist.
